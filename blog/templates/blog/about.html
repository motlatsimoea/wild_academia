About Us

Wild Academia(Wild-A) is an online platform that helps students find academic and career related support. We create a network between student – student, student – tutor/teacher, and student – mentor. This network allows the:

  • Student a platform to find tutors, and possibly even tutors within their area;
  • Students themselves to help each other with subjects they might be struggling to understand. Students can post questions for discussion with other students;
  • Individuals (teachers, graduates, and people who have completed high school) with an interest in tutoring to find students to assist;
  • Professionals in different career fields to share career advice, information about their professions, and to mentor prospective university students.
Our founding story

Wild-A was created in response to online learning during the Covid-19 pandemic. At the peak of the pandemic, students were forced to study from home and through social media platforms. This meant less physical contact with other students. It also meant that some students would struggle and need extra help as they would struggle to adjust to the dynamics of non-face-to-face learning. As a result, Wild-A was conceived.

Wild-A is also a response to limited realistic information on how to join certain professions. Students’ view of different professions can be quite limited and unrealistic. As a result some regret their career choices, some later learn of career choices that would have best suit them, and some only realised while in University just how much it takes to become that professional. So why not equip them with all this information before they make their choice? Why not help them make an informed decision?
